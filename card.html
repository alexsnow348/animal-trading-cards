Unicorn

Take a foray into the realm of magic with a deep dive into the mythical world of unicorns.

  • Other name: Monocerus
  • Grouping: Mythology
  • Symbol: Queer icon
  • Famous as: a symbol of purity and grace

Here are few fascinating facts about unicorns: unicorns are earthbound, unicorns are small but mighty, unicorns stick together, they are solar powered, and unicorns tend to have blue or purple eyes.
